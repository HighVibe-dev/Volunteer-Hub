Design a world-class, production-ready login page for an NGO Volunteer Management Platform called **"Volunteer Hub"** by **NayePankh Foundation**. The design should feel like a premium SaaS product built by a senior UI/UX designer from Stripe, Airbnb, Notion, or LinkedIn. The goal is to create an emotional connection with volunteers while maintaining a professional, trustworthy, and modern interface. Use a split-screen layout with the left side occupying approximately 60% of the screen and the right side 40%.

On the left side, create a visually inspiring hero section with a modern orange-to-amber gradient background (#FF7A18 to #FFB347) enhanced with subtle mesh gradients, floating particles, abstract shapes, and soft lighting effects. Place the NayePankh Foundation logo inside a floating glassmorphism card with a soft shadow and backdrop blur. Below the logo, display a powerful headline such as **"Creating Change Together"** and a supporting message like **"Join hundreds of volunteers making a real difference in communities across India through meaningful social impact initiatives."** Add a beautiful illustration or artwork showing volunteers engaged in community service activities such as teaching children, tree plantation drives, food distribution, environmental cleanup, and helping underprivileged communities. Include animated impact statistics presented as premium glass cards with hover effects: **500+ Active Volunteers**, **120+ Community Events**, **10,000+ Volunteer Hours**, and **50+ Partner Organizations**. Below the statistics, include a small testimonial carousel featuring volunteer success stories with profile images, names, and quotes. Also add a small upcoming-event widget displaying the next event name, date, location, and registration status. The hero section should feel inspiring, energetic, and community-driven.

On the right side, design a premium authentication card with a modern glassmorphism effect, white semi-transparent background, subtle border, large border radius (24px), soft shadows, and elegant spacing. At the top, display a welcoming heading such as **"Welcome Back 👋"** followed by the text **"Continue your volunteering journey and make a difference today."** Create modern input fields with icons inside them, smooth focus animations, subtle glow effects, and clear labels. Include an email field with a mail icon and a password field with a lock icon and a show/hide password toggle. Add a "Remember Me" checkbox and a "Forgot Password?" link aligned professionally. Include a prominent primary sign-in button with a vibrant orange-to-amber gradient, hover lift effect, shadow enhancement, smooth transitions, loading states, and click animations.

Below the sign-in button, add social authentication options including **Continue with Google** and **Continue with LinkedIn**, styled as premium buttons with logos and hover effects. Separate them from the email login using an elegant divider with the text **"OR CONTINUE WITH EMAIL"**. Include trust indicators such as **Verified NGO Platform**, **Secure Volunteer Data Protection**, **Trusted by 500+ Volunteers**, and **Government Registered Organization**, each accompanied by professional icons. Below the form, add a visually prominent registration section with the text **"Don't have an account?"** and a standout button saying **"Create Volunteer Account"**.

Use modern typography with excellent hierarchy, generous whitespace, smooth animations, micro-interactions, hover states, focus states, page transition effects, animated counters, floating cards, and responsive behavior. Ensure full accessibility compliance including proper color contrast, keyboard navigation, screen-reader support, and responsive layouts for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. On mobile devices, stack all sections vertically while maintaining visual appeal and usability. Use React, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Lucide Icons, and Shadcn UI components. The final design should feel like a premium NGO SaaS platform used by thousands of volunteers worldwide, significantly exceeding the quality of a typical student project and suitable for inclusion in a professional software engineering portfolio.
